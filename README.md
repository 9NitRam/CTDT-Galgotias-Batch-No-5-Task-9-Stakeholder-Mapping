## Topic Chosen

**Mobile Phones of a Company to be Upgraded with 5G**

## Objective

To identify and analyze all stakeholders involved in the 5G mobile phone upgrade project and understand their roles, interests, and influence.

## Main Project

**5G Mobile Phone Upgrade Project**

## Internal Stakeholders

* Company Management
* Product Development Team
* Software Engineers
* Hardware Engineers
* Marketing Team
* Customer Support Team

## External Stakeholders

* Mobile Users / Customers
* Telecom Service Providers
* 5G Network Equipment Suppliers
* Government & Regulatory Authorities
* Retailers and Distributors
* Investors

## Stakeholder Interests

* Faster Internet Speed
* Better Network Connectivity
* Improved Customer Experience
* Increased Sales
* Market Expansion

## Stakeholder Influence

### High Influence

* Company Management
* Telecom Operators
* Government Authorities

### Medium Influence

* Marketing Team
* Retailers
* Investors

### Low Influence

* End Users
* Suppliers

## Expected Outcomes

* Successful 5G rollout
* Increased customer satisfaction
* Higher revenue
* Competitive advantage
